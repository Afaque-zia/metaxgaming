Mission

The MetaXgaming team will develop planet "MetaX" where players can own virtual land, design and create various buildings and structures, fight for survival and craft a diverse set of items to be sold and traded on the open markets as NFTs.

The MetaXgaming team will develop a set of in-game market systems separately in which user can freely generate NFTs and can easily sell them at in game NFT marketplace. MetaXgaming team will also develop MMO like Battlegrounds, Racing games and Arena war games where players will experience the real thrill playing with their Avatar and winners will be rewarded with MXG token.
